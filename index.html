Why this map exists

📍

They're hidden in plain sight

Street libraries, toy libraries, seed swaps — they're everywhere, but unless you already know where they are, you walk straight past them. This map surfaces them all.

🤝

Community-powered accuracy

Libraries get installed, moved and removed all the time. Report updates and the map gets more accurate every week — powered by the people who actually use these spaces.

💰

Save money, reduce waste

A toy library membership can save a family hundreds a year. Seed libraries mean free food from your garden. Street libraries mean never buying another paperback you'll read once.

🌏

Open data, no tracking

Built on OpenStreetMap community data. No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Just a map that helps you find what your community shares.

Part of the family

Find the park. Confirm the change table. Check the dog's welcome. Grab a toy from the library two streets away. One good thing at a time.
